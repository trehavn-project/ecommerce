# Trehavn  
## American Woodcraft Studio Website

---

## Overview

Trehavn is an American artisan wood studio specializing in handcrafted home products designed with natural materials and timeless simplicity.

This website has been custom-developed to support the studio’s independent brand presence while complementing its Etsy marketplace operations.

The system reflects the studio’s commitment to craftsmanship, authenticity and premium quality positioning in the U.S. market.

---

## Brand Context

Trehavn operates as a boutique woodcraft studio offering:

- Handcrafted wooden home décor  
- Functional household wood products  
- Small-batch artisan production  
- Minimal, natural design aesthetics  

The website functions as a dedicated brand environment, strengthening authority and long-term customer relationships beyond marketplace dependency.

---

## Commerce Strategy

The business follows a dual-channel structure:

- Etsy Marketplace → audience reach and transactional exposure  
- Independent Website → brand storytelling, positioning control and future scalability  

This repository represents the structured web presence supporting that strategy.
